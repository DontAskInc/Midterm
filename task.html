Cheese Sauce!

If you want a nice, smooth cheese sauce for Mac+Cheese or Alfredo, here's the instructions to avoid that grainy texture or clumps of cheese!

Instructions

  1. Get a pan, and put on the stove over medium-low heat.
  2. Put in about 2tbs butter or ghee, and let it melt.
  3. Add a cup of Milk or Heavy cream to the pan, bringing to a light simmer.
  4. Add a teaspoon of Sodium Citrate. This is important, as sodium citrate prevents the protiens in cheese from clumping.
  5. Slowly add about a cup of whatever cheese you'd like, stirring constantly
  6. Once melted, season to taste and enjoy.
